About Us

We're helping women live better lives.

Binyoh is a platform that provides tailored sexual and reproductive health content to African women and also a safe space for them to interact with other women and health professionals.

Our Mission

To give African girls and women easy and reliable access to top quality sexual and reproductive health education, information, care and tools.

Our Vision

To help African women live their best lives by eliminating their health as another point of worry and to be identified with the very best in female healthcare technology for African women.

Giving African women easy and reliable access to top quality sexual and reproductive health education, information, care, tools, etc.
